NEAPOLITAN PIZZA



Neapolitan Pizza is a traditional Italian pizza originating from Naples. It features a thin, soft crust, simple toppings, and is baked at very high temperatures. The classic version is topped with tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough ball
  • 1/2 cup crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • 4 oz (115 g) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt, to taste

Steps to follow:

  1. Preheat the oven to its highest temperature, preferably with a pizza stone.
  2. Stretch the dough into a 10-12 inch round.
  3. Spread the crushed tomatoes evenly over the dough.
  4. Add the mozzarella slices and a pinch of salt.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil.
  6. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until the crust is puffed and lightly charred.
  7. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  8. Serve immediately.
