SUSHI



Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made with seasoned rice and a variety of ingredients such as raw fish, seafood, vegetables, and seaweed. It is known for its fresh flavors, elegant presentation, and wide range of styles, including nigiri, maki, and sashimi.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 8 oz (225 g) sushi-grade salmon or tuna
  • 1 cucumber, sliced into strips
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Soy sauce, for serving
  • Pickled ginger, for serving
  • Wasabi, for serving

Steps to follow:

  1. Rinse the sushi rice until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
  3. Mix the rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then fold it into the warm rice.
  4. Allow the rice to cool to room temperature.
  5. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat.
  6. Spread a thin layer of rice evenly over the nori.
  7. Add strips of fish, cucumber, and avocado near one edge.
  8. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat.
  9. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces with a sharp knife.
  10. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.
