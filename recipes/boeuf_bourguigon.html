BOEUF BOURGUIGON



Boeuf Bourguignon is a classic French stew made with beef slowly braised in red wine along with vegetables, herbs, and bacon. The long cooking process creates a rich, flavorful sauce and tender meat that melts in your mouth.

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs (900 g) beef chuck, cut into cubes
  • 4 slices bacon, chopped
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 8 oz (225 g) mushrooms, sliced

Steps to follow:

  1. Cook the bacon in a large pot until crisp. Remove and set aside.
  2. Brown the beef cubes in the bacon fat.
  3. Add the onion, carrots, and garlic. Cook until softened.
  4. Stir in the flour and tomato paste.
  5. Pour in the red wine and beef broth, stirring well.
  6. Add the thyme, bay leaves, bacon, salt, and pepper.
  7. Cover and simmer on low heat for 2 to 3 hours until the beef is tender.
  8. Add the mushrooms during the last 30 minutes of cooking.
  9. Remove the bay leaves and serve hot.
